Welcome to the webpage where you can calculate how similar your texts are.

This algorithm is a simplified version of Bilingual Evaluation Understudy (BLEU). It will return a number between 0 and 1, 0 as completely different and 1 as identical.

The genreral idea is to compare two texts by different length of words in order. For example, it will first compare using 1-gram(one word at a time), then 2-gram(two words at a time).
